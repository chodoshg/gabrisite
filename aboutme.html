About Me:

I graduated summa cum laude from the University of Oregon's honors college with a degree in business. My thesis was titled, "Innovating in University Career Services: Social Capital and Generalized Social Exchange."

Upon graduating, I was one of the first employees at a tech start-up called Nulia. I helped them put together a business plan for their $2M seed raise.

I then transitioned into consulting and started working on freelance projects in my spare time.

I work extensively with On Your Feet, a consultancy in town that works with major brands like Nike, Disney and Intel to design and faciliate interactive corporate training, culture development and ideation experiences.

Project Highlights

Design-Thinking: Persona Development for a $95 Million Renovation

I led a team of 4 consultants in a design-thinking project that involved online survey development & deployment, interviews, focus groups and persona development. Our deliverable helped influence the $95 million renovation plans of the University of Oregon's Erb Memorial Union. Read more here.

Transforming a Brand: A Company's Steward-Ownership Viability

Alongside a VP of Finance and former Director of Growth, I modified a complex valuation model for a client looking to transition their business to a steward-ownership model. Our deliverable included the financial model and a recommended exit scenario that would make the ownership transition feasible.


Mapping an Ecosystem: Moving VertueLab from Spreadsheets to Airtable

I worked with the Director of Impact Investing at a non-profit to help transfer their customer relationship database (which was in Google Sheets) to a new CRM system called Airtable. In the process, I updated and cleaned the data and provided strategic guidance regarding who they should contact.
